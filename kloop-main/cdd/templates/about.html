About Us

In 2017 GC, a small company was formed in Ethiopia with a little more than passion and the desire to
add something to the Ethiopian construction and interior design landscape. humble corner office,
a seed was planted which eventually grew to become the enterprise whose mission entailed beautifying
the spaces we reside in whilst productively adding value to customer lifestyle in the process. Today
AMCO has been known for its quality and timely delivery for the job we offered, applying the main aim
remains true to the initial purpose of creating quality customized living and working spaces. Our
portfolio includes executive offices, mixed buildings, residential houses, and a bank. We are also
involved in developing smarter office and living spaces – spaces that speak to us intelligently
by making use of new insights. This is our contribution to a smarter planet. Changing homes and lives
has never been more rewarding.

Vision

A competitive market player in provision of civil engineering and building construction services

Mission

We aim to be a world class engineering Construction Company providing efficient affordable, high aesthetic value, sustainable, cost- effective services of highest level of quality though commitment and responsibility.

Value

We believe in maintaining the highest standard of professionalism, integrity, creativity, positive attitude, delivery that meets and surpass expectations while offering prompt and lasting solutions that stand the test of time.

Objectives

Delivering projects on time and within budget, ensuring client satisfaction. Continuously improving our processes to enhance efficiency and effectiveness. Fostering a safe and healthy work environment for our employees and stakeholders.

Core Competencies

Expertise in innovative design and construction techniques. Strong project management capabilities ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery.

Customer Commitment

Providing personalized services tailored to their specific needs and goals. Ensuring transparent communication and collaboration throughout the project lifecycle. Offering post-project support and maintenance to ensure ongoing satisfaction and project success.
